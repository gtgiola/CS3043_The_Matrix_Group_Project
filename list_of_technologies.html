List of Technologies in The Matrix

  • Hardwired Phone - March 10, 18761
  • Wiretapping - 19282
    • Wiretapping began shortly after the invention of the telephone. This date, 1928, corresponds with the first court case that used wiretapping as a means for conviction.
  • Programmable Computers - 19363
  • Encrypted Messages - 1940s4
  • Hovercraft - 19565
    • Although hovercrafts like the Nebuchadnezzar do not yet exist, hovercrafs today are supported by a cushion of air.
  • Artificial Intelligence - 19566
  • Autonomous Machines (Robots) - 19617
  • Cellular Phone - 1973 8
  • Electromagnetic Pulse - 1980s9,10
    • The electromagnetic pulse was discovered during nuclear testing. However, it was not until the 1980s that scientists began researching this particular effect
  • Rebuilding Muscle Tissue - December 1, 201111
    • As of 2011, scientists have been able to rebuild muscle tissue in mice. This technology is not available for humans yet, but it is still being researched.
  • Implanting chips on humans - 199812,13
  • Chip removal tool - 199812,13
  • Human Storage/Growing. Human Power Source
    • Fictional. Not currently being researched.
  • Loading Software Programs into Human Mind
    • Computer assisted learning is possibly the first step.
    • The technology as depicted in the movie is very far away or impossible.
    • Fictional. Not currently being researched.
  • Computer Generated Dream World ("The Matrix")
    • Much farther than virtual reality.
    • Fictional. Not currently being researched.
  • Teletransportation
    • Transportation into and out of the Matrix through phone lines.
    • Agents can take over human bodies to effectively teleport.
    • Fictional. Not currently being researched.
