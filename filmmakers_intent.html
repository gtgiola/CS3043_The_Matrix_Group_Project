Filmmakers' Intent

  • Portray modern dependence on machines
    • Currently machines are taking jobs that would previously be held by humans.
    • In the film, machines and humans have switched roles. Machines control the system and interactions within it, while humans are subservient batteries.
  • Raise awareness about the importance of exhaustive code testing
    • Bugs in code could lead to catastrophe
  • Suggest that humans be cautious about what technology can become
    • In the movie, machines have basically become the dominant race
